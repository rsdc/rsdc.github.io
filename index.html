Open Designs

We are a group of professionals and enthusiasts pushing the state of the industry in software design, technical support, operations, and quality of service.

As creative individuals we seek out new and improved ways of solving problems using the most advanced tools and materials available.

Our designs and workflows are open for collaboration, and we encourage engineers and hobbyists alike to build on our tools and SDKs for both commercial and noncommercial use.
